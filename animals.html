Afgan Hound

The Afghan Hound is a hound that is distinguished by its thick, fine, silky coat and its tail with a ring curl at the end. The breed is selectively bred for its unique features in the cold mountains of Afghanistan.

Desert tortoise

The desert tortoise (Gopherus agassizii), is a species of tortoise in the family Testudinidae.

Key Deer

The Key deer (Odocoileus virginianus clavium) is an endangered deer that lives only in the Florida Keys. It is a subspecies of the white-tailed deer (O. virginianus). It is the smallest known extant North American deer species.
